Spaceship Simulator

Starting game

Click on the reload button on the simulator to start, and if the butttons on the simulator goes gray, hover over them, and continue playing.

Instructions

  • Use the arrow keys to move around
  • Use the space bar to fire a power pellet
  • Shoot the space junk with the pellets
  • Avoid hiting junk
  • Colect hearts, but don't shoot them! If you do, you destory the heart and can't collect it. Also, if you shoot one, you loose a point.

About the game

This game is a game where you are a U.S. Space Force astronut destroying space junk around earth in the year 2054, when there is too much space junk in orbit of earth due to alot of rocket launches.
Your goal is to destory as much space junk as you can without crashing your spaceship.
